sparvk kickstarter is a crash course that teaches you how to effectively use opensource operating systems in you daily work life.

Who this course is for?

sparvk kicstarter course is for anyone who wants to learn what an opensource operating system is. Whether you are an individual or a business, a student or a professional this kickstarter course helps you understand how an opensource operating system can help you increase your productivity and reduce the amount of money you have to spend on proprietary software.

If you're the owner of a business interested in trying out opensource operating systems to reduce your IT costs but don't feel convinced that it can replace/augment your existing systems then this course will give you all the information you need to reach a decision.

If you're a system administrator then this course will teach you how to create standby systems that you can use to make sure that the IT infrastructure in your organization is always available even if hardware components fail.

If you are a student who can't pay the high licensing fees of proprietary software this course will teach you how to replace the paid software with opensource programs.

If you're are a hardware manufacturer/ repair shop this course will teach you how you can package your hardware services together with opensource software to provide greater value to your customers.

What will you learn from this course?

  1. Installation of the system
  2. The steps you need to follow to create users and administrators for the system
  3. A general overview of what can be achieved on the operating system.
  4. A quick run down of common tasks
    • Starting and stopping the system. An overview of the concepts of sessions and users.
    • Connecting to the internet and browsing on the internet
    • Creating files and directories
    • Running programs.
    • How to perform common business tasks like printing files, working with spreadsheets, presentations etc ?
  5. How to install new packages in the system?
  6. How to backup the files and folders in the system?
  7. The modes of system operation. Operating the system from the GUI and from the terminal. A few words on system logs
  8. A discussion on Industry specific use cases.
  9. Troubleshooting the system. How to solve problems on your own? How to take the help of community?
  10. Closing remarks on opensource software and how it has shaped the computer industry?

Our course is designed to be beginner friendly

sparvk kickstarter course is designed to help to organizations make most effective use of IT. It can help to reskill your workforce to learn how to use the best tools available in the market today. Most of all the kickstarter course is designed to teach you new way of thinking about computers for you and your organization.
